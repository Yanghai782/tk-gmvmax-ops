Rates sourced from TikTok Shop Seller University official pages (accessed 2026-06-27).
Exact rates depend on product category. Values below are the default/typical ranges.

## Important Notes

### Mall vs Non-Mall
All 5 markets have separate rate tables for Mall and non-Mall sellers.
Mall rates are typically 0.5-2% higher than non-Mall.

### Category Dependency
Commission rates vary significantly by product subcategory.
Default rates apply to categories not listed in the detailed rate tables.
Check the official rate table for your exact product category.

### How Fees Feed Into Break-Even ROI

```
Total_Fee_Rate = Platform_Commission_% + Transaction_Fee_%
BreakEven_ROI = Price / (Price - Product_Cost - Price * Total_Fee_Rate)
```

### Thailand GMV Max Commission Waiver
Thailand offers 1.07% commission for non-Mall sellers whose GMV Max ad spend
exceeds 3% of platform revenue over the past 30 days. This is a significant discount.
