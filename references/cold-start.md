# Cold Start Playbook

## What Is Cold Start?

Getting a product from 0 to stable delivery on GMV Max.
The system needs to learn: which users respond to which creatives for which product.

## The Four Phases

1. **Exploration** (0 to ~50 conversions) — System tests audiences, CPM/CPA high and volatile
2. **Stabilization** (~50+ conversions) — CPA gradually stabilizes
3. **Scaling** — System confidently matches product to audiences, lower CPA
4. **Decay** — Creatives fatigue, need fresh material

Full cycle typically takes 3-7 days.

## Day 1: Launch

### Goal
Feed the system data. Do NOT optimize for ROI.

### Setup
- **Budget**: Target_CPA * 50 (minimum to reach 50 conversions)
- **Bid**: Target_CPA * 1.2 ~ 1.5 (leave room for exploration)
- **Audience**: Broad. Do not narrow by demographics or interests. System learns best from wide pool.
- **Creatives**: 3-5 different angles, all targeting the same product

### What To Watch
- Is it spending? If not spending at all, bid may be too low or creative rejected
- Volume of impressions
- First conversions coming in
- Ignore ROI completely on Day 1

### Common Day 1 Mistake
Setting audience too narrow. Your guess about who will buy is NOT the same
as who the system will actually find. Let the system explore.

## Day 2: Observe

### Goal
Let learning continue. Begin identifying early winners.

### What To Watch
- Which creatives are getting the most budget allocation? (ECPM winners)
- CPA starting to trend down?
- Any creatives with zero conversions despite decent spend?

### Actions
- If a creative has 3+ days with 0 conversions: kill it
- If CPM is wildly above CPM_cap and not trending down: consider killing
- Do NOT pause/restart campaigns (resets learning)
- Do NOT narrow audience yet
- Do NOT change bid unless completely not spending
- Add 1-2 new creatives to test alongside existing ones

### Day 2 Mindset
High ROI on Day 2 doesn't mean winning (may be sampling luck).
Low ROI on Day 2 doesn't mean losing (may still be exploring).
Judge by volume and direction, not by ROI.

## Day 3+: Optimize

### Goal
Confirm winners, eliminate losers, begin measuring ROI.

### Actions
- Kill any creative with 0 conversions over 3 days
- Kill creatives with CPM consistently above CPM_cap
- Winning creative emerges? Can now support multiple similar creatives
- CPA stabilizing? Begin lowering bid toward target CPA

### After Learning Phase (50+ conversions, ~3-7 days)
- CPA is stable → start watching ROI closely
- Can begin lowering bid toward target CPA
- Can begin modest audience narrowing
- Continue adding 1-2 new creatives daily
- Creative lifespan typically 2-4 weeks before decay

## Success Criteria

[OK] System spending steadily (5+ orders/day)
[OK] CPA within acceptable range
[OK] 1-2 creatives identified as winners
[OK] Exit learning phase (50+ conversions accumulated)

## The Golden Rule

The goal of cold start is to find the winning creative.
One winning creative can carry an entire product.
The system is your ally — feed it, don't fight it.
